# 🎯 osu!helper

**Find your next favourite beatmap.** osu!helper analyses your top plays and recommends similar maps you haven't played yet — and fires off a fresh set of recommendations the moment you set a new score.

🌐 **[osuhelper.com](https://osuhelper.com)**

---

## How to use it

1. Head to **[osuhelper.com](https://osuhelper.com)**
2. Click **Login with osu!** — uses your existing osu! account, no sign-up needed
3. Your top plays load automatically
4. Switch to the **Recommendations** tab to see maps picked for you

That's it.

---

## Features

**Top Plays** — your top 100 scores displayed with cover art, PP, accuracy, mods, AR, BPM and length at a glance.

**Recommendations** — maps chosen based on your taste profile: the star rating, AR, BPM and style of what you actually perform best on. Pulls from both the osu! catalogue and [nerinyan.moe](https://nerinyan.moe).

**Similar to a specific map** — click the 🎯 **Similar** button on any play to get recommendations tailored to that exact map instead of your overall profile.

**Live new top play detection** — the app polls your scores in the background. The moment you set a new score in your top 20, a notification pops up and a brand new set of recommendations loads automatically.

**Multi-profile support** — running the desktop app locally? Add multiple osu! profiles and switch between them from the header.

---

## Why did it recommend this map?

Each recommendation card shows a short reason, e.g. *"very similar difficulty (6.8★) · same AR (9.5) · similar BPM (220)"* so you know exactly why it was picked.

---

## Desktop app

Prefer to run it offline? Grab the source, drop in your osu! API credentials and launch it as a native desktop app — no browser needed.
